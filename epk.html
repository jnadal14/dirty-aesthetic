Electronic Press Kit

Dirty Aesthetic is indie garage rock from Vancouver. Formed in 2024, the band blends raw hooks with sepia stained grit. Themes run through heartbreak, love, addiction, mental health, and good times.

Lineup

  • Ethan Billings, keyboards
  • Josh Chapman, drums
  • Dylan Iwaschuck, lead vocals
  • Jacob Nadal, rhythm guitar, backing vocals
  • Josh Seaman, bass
  • Bardia Tarjoman, lead guitar
