Electronic Press Kit

Formed in Greater Vancouver in the summer of 2024, Dirty Aesthetic delivers surf garage infused indie rock layered with folk and alternative textures. Their sound moves effortlessly from high energy tracks that get crowds dancing to slow, heartfelt songs that pull at the heartstrings. Lyrically, they explore themes of heartbreak, love, mental health, and addiction.
